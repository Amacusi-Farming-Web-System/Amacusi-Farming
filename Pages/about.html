Our Farming Story

Rooted in tradition, growing for the future

Who We Are

Amacusi Farming is a proudly South African agricultural enterprise founded in 2015 by Qiniso Khumalo in Mpumalanga. What began as a small family poultry operation has grown into a trusted supplier of quality meat and eggs to restaurants and businesses across the region.

We stand for ethical farming practices, sustainable growth, and delivering farm-fresh products with uncompromising quality.

Our Mission

To nourish communities through sustainable farming while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To become a leader in ethical meat production in Southern Africa, pioneering methods that respect both animals and ecosystems.

Our Sustainable Practices

Free-Range Poultry

Our chickens enjoy daily outdoor access with:

  • 8+ hours of sunlight and foraging daily
  • Supplemented with non-GMO grains
  • No growth hormones or routine antibiotics

Grass-Fed Beef

Our cattle thrive on:

  • Native Mpumalanga grasses
  • Seasonal pasture rotation
  • Mineral licks (no growth promoters)

Waste Recycling

Nothing goes to waste:

  • Manure composted for vegetable gardens
  • Eggshells crushed for chicken calcium
  • Rainwater harvesting for animal drinking

Ethical Egg Production

Our hens lay eggs naturally:

  • Straw-lined nesting boxes
  • 16 hours of natural daylight
  • Hand-collected twice daily
